🔧 Setup Required

To get this trip planner online, you need a (free) Firebase project:

  1. Go to Firebase Console
  2. Create a new project (disable Google Analytics — not needed)
  3. Go to Build → Authentication → Sign-in method and enable Anonymous
  4. Go to Build → Firestore Database and create a database (start in test mode)
  5. Go to Project Settings → Your apps → Add Web app
  6. Copy the firebaseConfig object
  7. Paste the values into the FIREBASE_CONFIG object in index.html

See README.md for the full guide including Firestore security rules and GitHub Pages deployment.

💧 Water

⚠️#1 concern in late summer/early autumn. Streams may be low or dry after summer. Confirm conditions with CapeNature when collecting your permit. Fill up at every flowing source. Carry 3–4L capacity minimum.

🔥 Fire

🚫Open fires are prohibited in the wilderness area year-round. Stove only. No exceptions.

🐒 Baboons

🐵Can be bold. Never leave food unattended. Hang or secure food bags at night and when leaving camp (Day 3 day trip).

🐍 Snakes

👀Cape cobra and puff adder are present. Watch where you step and place your hands, especially on rocky terrain.

🧭 Navigation

🗺️Trails are loosely marked with cairns and paint blazes. The plateau can be confusing in mist. Carry your topo map AND phone with offline AllTrails routes. Cell signal is virtually nonexistent.

🎨 Rock Art

🖐️Ancient San rock art sites are found throughout the Cederberg. Look but don't touch — oils from skin damage the paintings. Don't use flash photography.

🚗 Car Logistics

🔑You're carpooling with the crew from Kommetjie. If Groenvlei and Grasvlei are different trailheads, you may need a shuttle between them — check with the farm (Sanddrif/Dwarsrivier). Monday night is a ~3.5 hr drive back to Kommetjie, so finish hiking with enough daylight.

⛽ Gas Canister

✈️Cannot be carried on flights. Buy your gas canister in Cape Town before driving up. Cape Union Mart and Outdoor Warehouse stock them.

📦 Weight

⚖️Expect ~18–20 kg per person with full water. Share tent weight. Day 3 will feel glorious with just a day pack!
